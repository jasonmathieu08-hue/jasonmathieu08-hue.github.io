Why Pineapple Pizza is Good?

Okay, hear me out—pineapple on pizza might sound weird at first, but it’s actually amazing. the sweet pineapple balances out the salty, cheesy goodness of the pizza. It’s that perfect sweet-and-salty combo that hits all the right spots. You get the savory taste from the cheese and ham, and you also get this tropical sweetness of pineapple that puts it all together
